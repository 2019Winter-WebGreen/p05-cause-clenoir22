What is the SPCA?

The SPCA stands for Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals and is a nonprofit organization that hepls save animals lives and gives them happy, loving families to care for and adopt them. The SPCA is not a national organization but they are all over the United States. Thye rely on the help from you and your donations to fund their program since they do not recieve federal or state funding. You can also help out by volenteering at the local spot in your community.

How to Help Out!

There are so many ways you can help out at the SPCA. You can volunteer, donate,or even adopt. When you donate you can donate your money towards lots of things. One of them being the Give a Paw a Hand Fund, which means your money is going towards spaying, neutering, and vacinations that the animals need to live a happy, safe life.
